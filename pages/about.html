Main Cost Factors

Size (Length × Width)

Bigger size increases material consumption.

Bag Weight / GSM

Higher GSM = higher cost + strength.

Fabric Colour

White & milky cost higher than recycled.

Fabric Strength

Higher denier & mesh increases durability.

Filler / Ash %

Higher filler reduces cost but reduces strength.

Lamination

Natural / milky lamination adds moisture protection.

Printing

More colors = higher cost.

BOPP

Premium look with high-quality print.

Liner

Extra protection for food & chemicals.

Cost Formula

Cost = (Weight × Raw Material Rate) + Printing + Lamination + BOPP + Liner + Conversion
