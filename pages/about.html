Our Manufacturing Process

Every bag is made through a controlled multi-step process that ensures consistent strength, accurate dimensions and clear print quality in every batch.

1

Raw Material Selection

We use 100% virgin polypropylene granules sourced from trusted Indian polymer manufacturers ensuring consistent melt flow, fabric strength and food grade compliance.

2

Tape Extrusion

Virgin PP granules are melted and extruded through precision dies to form flat PP tapes. GSM and fabric strength are determined at this stage.

3

Circular Weaving

PP tape is fed into high speed circular looms producing seamless tubular fabric. Loom speed, weave density and fabric width are monitored continuously.

4

Lamination (For BOPP Bags)

BOPP film is heat bonded to the outer fabric surface. Glossy, matte, gold or silver finish applied here creating smooth surface for high definition printing.

5

Flexographic Printing

Custom designs printed using flexographic machines in up to 6 colors on PP fabric or up to 8 colors on BOPP laminated surface.

6

Cutting & Stitching

Fabric rolls cut to precise dimensions then stitched into final bag shape with bottom stitching, top hemming and valve or liner fitting as needed.

7

Quality Inspection

Every batch inspected for dimensional accuracy, stitching strength, print quality and fabric defects. Only approved bags are packed and dispatched.

8

Packing & Dispatch

Finished bags bundled, counted and packed in bales or boxes as per buyer requirement. Dispatched through trusted logistics partners pan India.
