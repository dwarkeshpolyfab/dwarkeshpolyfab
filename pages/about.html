Manufacturing Process

From PP Granules to Finished Packaging Bags

A buyer-friendly view of how raw material becomes strong, printable and market-ready polypropylene packaging.

01

Material Selection

PP granules, masterbatch and additives selected according to bag strength, color, GSM and application requirement.

02

Tape Extrusion

PP is converted into flat tapes. Tape quality impacts fabric strength, weight consistency and final bag performance.

03

Fabric Weaving

Tapes are woven into PP fabric rolls as per required width, GSM and strength parameters.

04

Lamination

Natural, milky or BOPP lamination can be applied for moisture resistance, premium look and print performance.

05

Printing

Buyer logo, product name, weight, design and instructions printed as per approved artwork.

06

Cutting

Fabric rolls are cut to the required bag length and width with batch consistency checks.

07

Stitching

Bags are stitched, hemmed, liner-fitted or finished according to product and filling method.

08

Packing & Dispatch

Finished bags are counted, bundled, packed and shipped with buyer communication until dispatch.
