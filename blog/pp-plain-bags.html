What Are PP Plain Bags?

PP plain bags are woven sacks made from polypropylene tapes. Unlike BOPP laminated or multicolor printed bags, plain PP bags have a simple woven fabric finish and are mainly selected for strength, low cost and fast production.

These bags can be supplied as natural white, milky white or colored plain fabric. Buyers can also choose basic one-color printing for name, weight, batch details or handling instructions.

Size & GSM Guide for PP Plain Bags

The right PP plain bag depends on product weight, filling method, transport handling and stacking condition. A low GSM bag may save money, but it can create product loss if the bag tears during loading or warehouse movement.

PP Plain Bags vs Printed PP Bags vs BOPP Bags

PP plain bags are not always the final answer for every product. If your buyer needs branding, retail appearance or premium shelf display, printed PP bags or BOPP laminated bags may be better. But for bulk commodity movement, plain PP bags are usually the most practical option.

When Do PP Plain Bags Need Liner or Lamination?

Plain PP woven fabric is strong, but for moisture-sensitive products, you should consider lamination or PE inner liner. This is important for products that absorb moisture, leak dust or require longer storage.

  • Choose non-laminated plain PP bags for dry, low-risk, fast-moving bulk goods.
  • Choose laminated PP plain bags when the product needs basic moisture protection and a better surface finish.
  • Choose PP plain bags with liner for fine powder, food grain, fertilizer, chemicals and moisture-sensitive material.
  • Choose printed PP bags if dealer branding or product identification is required.
