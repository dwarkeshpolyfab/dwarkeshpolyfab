Best Bags for Biomass & Biofuel Pellets (25kg & 50kg)

25kg and 50kg biomass pellet packaging bags used in biofuel industry

With the rapid growth of green energy and biofuel industries in India, biomass pellet manufacturers are facing one key challenge — reliable and cost-effective packaging.

Biomass pellets are highly sensitive to moisture and require strong packaging to handle transportation, storage and export conditions.

---

Why Packaging is Important for Biomass Pellets?

  • Protects pellets from moisture damage
  • Prevents breakage during handling
  • Ensures safe transportation
  • Improves product quality and presentation
---

Best Bag Options for Biomass Packaging

1. PP Woven Bags (25kg & 50kg)

PP woven bags are the most widely used packaging solution for biomass pellets due to their strength and cost efficiency.

  • Strong enough for 25kg and 50kg loads
  • Cost-effective for bulk supply
  • Optional inner liner for moisture protection
