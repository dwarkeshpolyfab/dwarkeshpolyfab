Why 40kg Packing Works for Biomass Pellets

A 40kg biomass pellet bag can be easier to handle than a 50kg bag and more cost-effective than smaller 20kg or 25kg packs. For manufacturers selling to industrial users, boilers and distributors, 40kg often gives a practical balance between bag cost, labor handling and transport efficiency.

Better handling

40kg bags reduce lifting stress compared with heavier 50kg packaging.

Good bulk value

More economical per kg than small retail-style bags for industrial buyers.

Warehouse friendly

Works well for stacking when fabric, stitching and palletizing are selected correctly.

Moisture-Proof Protection for Valuable Biomass Pellets

Biomass pellets can lose quality when they absorb moisture. The product may swell, break, create dust or become difficult to burn efficiently. For valuable products, bag protection should be stronger than only plain PP woven fabric.

1

PP Fabric

Select fabric strength based on 40kg load and handling.

2

Milky Lamination

Add a surface barrier and clean white appearance.

3

PE Liner

Use liner for monsoon, export or longer storage.

4

Stitching

Strong bottom stitching reduces leakage risk.

5

Pallet Cover

Protect stacked bags from warehouse humidity and dust.

Practical note: Moisture-proof does not mean the bag should be kept in rain or water. Best protection comes from lamination, liner, correct stitching, pallet cover and dry warehouse storage together.
