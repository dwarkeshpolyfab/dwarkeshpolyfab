Why Indian Exporters Need Anti Slip Polybags for International Shipments

Anti slip bags are a small upgrade that prevents major cargo damage during sea and road transport. Here is everything Indian exporters need to know.

What is an Anti Slip Bag?

An anti slip bag is a standard polypropylene woven bag with a special coating applied to its outer surface. This coating creates friction between stacked bags on a pallet — preventing them from sliding over each other during transport. Without this coating, smooth polypropylene bags have very little friction and pallet loads can shift, topple or collapse during loading, sea freight and container unloading.

Why Regular PP Bags Fail in Export Shipments

Standard PP woven bags have a smooth fabric surface. When stacked 8 to 10 layers high on a pallet inside a container, the smooth surfaces offer little resistance against the natural movement of the vessel. During a sea voyage the container experiences constant motion — pitching, rolling and vibration. Over a long voyage this causes bags to shift sideways, lean and sometimes the entire pallet topples over inside the container.

The result is damaged bags, contaminated product, insurance claims and unhappy international buyers — all of which damage your export reputation and future orders.

How Anti Slip Coating Solves This

The anti slip coating increases the coefficient of friction between bag surfaces. This means each layer of bags grips the layer below it and the entire pallet remains stable even during rough sea conditions and road transport vibration. The coating does not affect the bag's strength, print quality or food safety — it only adds grip to the outside surface.

Real world benefit: Many Indian rice exporters switched to anti slip bags after facing cargo damage claims from Middle East and African buyers. After switching, cargo damage complaints dropped significantly and container loading became faster and more stable.

Anti Slip + BOPP Lamination — The Best Combination

For exporters who need both brand quality and cargo safety, the best solution is a BOPP laminated bag with anti slip coating on the outer surface. The BOPP layer provides moisture protection and premium print quality. The anti slip coating prevents pallet shifting. Together they give you a bag that looks professional, protects your product and satisfies international buyer requirements.

What to Tell Your Bag Manufacturer

  • Specify anti slip coating on the outer surface of the bag
  • Mention whether you need BOPP lamination combined with anti slip
  • Provide your bag dimensions and GSM requirement
  • Share any buyer country or port specific requirements
  • Ask for a sample before placing bulk order to test the grip
