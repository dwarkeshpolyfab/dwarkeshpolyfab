Capsacks Bags for Bulk Lightweight Packaging – Complete Industrial Guide

Capsacks are specially designed large size PP woven bags used for packaging bulky but lightweight products. They are widely used in industries where products take up more space but do not weigh much, making them highly cost-effective for transport and storage.

What Are Capsacks?

Capsacks are high-volume polypropylene bags designed to carry large quantities of lightweight items such as plastic products, toys, and small cartons. They are different from standard PP bags, which are mainly used for heavy materials.

Common Uses of Capsacks

  • Plastic jugs, buckets and containers
  • Toys and lightweight products
  • Small boxes and cartons
  • Industrial lightweight goods
  • Bulk packaging for logistics

Technical Specifications

  • Material: PP Woven Fabric
  • Size: Custom Large Sizes Available
  • GSM: 40–80 GSM
  • Lamination: Optional
  • Printing: Available
  • Stitching: Strong Bottom Stitch

Why Choose Capsacks?

Capsacks provide excellent value for industries dealing with bulky items. They allow more products per bag, reduce transportation cost, and improve storage efficiency.

  • Cost-effective packaging solution
  • High volume storage capacity
  • Easy to handle and transport
  • Custom size and design options

Capsacks are widely used for domestic supply and export packaging. They help optimize container space and reduce logistics cost, making them ideal for bulk shipments.
