What Are Capsacks?

Capsacks are made from woven polypropylene (PP) fabric and are designed for heavy-duty packaging. These bags are commonly used in agriculture, chemical, fertilizer and export industries.

  • High strength and durability
  • Reusable and cost-effective
  • Suitable for bulk packaging

Applications of Capsacks

  • Agriculture products packaging
  • Fertilizer and chemical industries
  • Export packaging solutions
  • Industrial bulk material handling

Why Choose PP Capsacks for Packaging?

  • Lightweight but strong material
  • Resistant to moisture and external damage
  • Ideal for long-distance transportation
  • Perfect for container export shipments
