What Changes BOPP Bag Price?

Only these points matter for most quotations. Keep them ready before asking final price.

1

Bag size

More width and length means more fabric, film and lamination.

2

Fabric GSM

Higher GSM improves strength but increases cost and weight.

3

Glossy or matt

Glossy and matt BOPP film have different finish rates.

4

Liner option

Natural, semi-natural or milky liner adds protection and cost.

5

Gusset

Side gusset improves filling shape and changes consumption.

6

Cylinder and MOQ

Printing cylinder, artwork and 10,000 bag MOQ affect final quote.

Glossy or Matt?

Both can look premium. Choose based on your brand style.

Glossy BOPP Bag

Best when you want bright colors, shine and quick market attention. Good for rice, wheat, cattle feed, fertilizer and retail agro products.

Matt BOPP Bag

Best when you want a premium, soft and less reflective look. Good for brands that want a modern printed packaging feel.
