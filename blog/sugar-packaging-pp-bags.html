Why Sugar Packaging Needs PP Bags with Milky Liner

Sugar is a moisture-sensitive product. If the packaging is weak, unlined or poorly stitched, sugar can absorb moisture, form lumps, leak dust during handling or lose its clean appearance in warehouse storage.

A milky PE liner is an inner liner used inside the PP woven bag. It creates a cleaner inner layer and helps reduce direct moisture contact. For sugar mills, traders and wholesale suppliers, liner bags are useful when sugar must travel long distances, stay in warehouse storage or move through multiple handling points.

What Is Hemming Stitching in Sugar PP Bags?

Hemming stitching means folding the edge of the bag and stitching it so the opening becomes stronger and cleaner. In sugar packaging, hemming is useful because it reduces loose threads, improves the mouth finish and makes the bag easier to fill in mill or warehouse operations.

For sugar bags with liner, the stitching style should be selected carefully. Buyers should confirm whether the liner is loose inserted, stitched with the bag, folded at the top, or sealed separately after filling. The best option depends on your filling process and moisture protection requirement.

Size and GSM Guide for Sugar Packaging Bags

The correct GSM depends on bag size, sugar weight, stacking height, liner thickness and transport handling. A low GSM bag may reduce cost, but if sugar is packed in 50kg and handled roughly, stronger fabric is safer.

Milky Liner, Natural Liner and Lamination Options

Buyers often ask whether sugar packaging needs liner, lamination or both. The answer depends on the sugar grade, moisture exposure, storage time and buyer quality requirement.

Milky PE Liner

Best for buyers who want a clean inner layer and better moisture protection inside the PP woven bag.

Natural Liner

Useful when the buyer wants basic product separation without the milky appearance.

Outer Lamination

Improves surface finish and gives extra protection for handling and storage.

BOPP Lamination

Best for retail sugar brands that need high-quality multicolor printing and shelf appeal.
