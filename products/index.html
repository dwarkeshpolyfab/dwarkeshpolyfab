How Buyers Choose the Right Bag

For the best quote, send us your bag size, GSM, weight capacity, printing requirement, quantity and delivery city.

For Food & Agriculture

Rice, grain, seed and animal feed buyers usually need strong PP woven fabric, good stitching, safe handling and clear printed branding.

For Cement & Chemicals

Cement, fertilizer and chemical buyers should focus on GSM, lamination, strength, moisture protection and stacking safety.

For Retail & Export

BOPP laminated bags help brands improve shelf appeal with multicolor printing, glossy or matte finish and export-friendly presentation.
