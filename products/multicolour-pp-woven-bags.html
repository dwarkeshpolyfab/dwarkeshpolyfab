Colour Configuration Options

Choose Your Colour Style

Multicolour PP bags are available in three colour configurations — each serving a different purpose in terms of cost, branding and product identification.

Premium
🎨

Full Colour Fabric

Complete Colour Throughout

  • Entire bag fabric is woven using coloured PP tapes — the colour runs through the full structure of the bag on all sides.
  • Maximum brand visibility — your brand colour is visible from every angle at the mandi, warehouse or retail point.
  • Strongest product identification — buyers recognise your brand instantly by colour even before reading the printing.
  • Available in single solid colour or two-colour combinations woven into the fabric structure.
  • Best choice for agricultural mandi bags where visual identification at distance is important.
Popular
🟦

Side Colour Strips

Coloured Edges on White Bag

  • White PP fabric bag with coloured strips woven into both side edges — a cost-effective colour identification solution.
  • Side strips allow the main white body to carry printed text, product details and barcodes with maximum readability.
  • Popular for chemical powder bags like dolomite and sulfur where product information clarity is more important than full colour.
  • Two or three different colours can be used in side strips to create a unique product identification code for different product grades.
  • Lower cost than full colour bags while still providing clear visual product differentiation on the shelf or at the mandi.
⬛

Center Colour Strip

Single Strip Down the Middle

  • A single bold colour strip woven down the center of the bag face — a simple, cost-efficient identification mark.
  • Most economical colour option — minimal coloured material used while still providing clear visual distinction between product types.
  • Widely used for commodity bags at mandis where multiple product grades need to be identified quickly during sorting and trading.
  • The centre strip does not interfere with printing — your brand text and product details can be printed clearly on both sides of the strip.
  • Can be combined with side strips for a more distinctive multi-stripe identification pattern.
Why Multicolour Bags

Why Businesses Choose Multicolour PP Bags

Colour in packaging is not just aesthetic — it is a functional tool for product identification, brand recognition and operational efficiency in agricultural markets, warehouses and industrial supply chains.

🏪

Instant Identification at Mandi

At busy agricultural market yards, colour-coded bags allow buyers, traders and weighing staff to identify your product immediately from a distance — speeding up trading and reducing handling errors.

🏷️

Brand Differentiation

Your brand's signature colour on the bag creates a visual identity that competitors cannot easily copy — building recognition among repeat buyers across multiple mandi seasons.

⚗️

Product Grade Coding

Chemical and mineral powder manufacturers use different bag colours or stripe combinations to identify product grades, concentrations or variants — eliminating costly mis-shipment errors in the supply chain.

♻️

Colour Built Into Fabric

Unlike printed colour that fades and scratches, multicolour PP bags have colour woven into the fabric structure itself — the colour remains vibrant through outdoor storage, monsoon season and repeated handling.

💪

Same Strength as White Bags

Coloured PP tapes have the same tensile strength as white PP tapes — multicolour bags carry the same load, perform the same way and last just as long as standard white PP woven bags.

✍️

Printable for Full Branding

Multicolour fabric bags can still be flexo-printed with your brand name, logo, weight, address and certifications — colour fabric plus print gives the maximum brand impact at the lowest total cost.

19×34 PP Bags for Chemical Powder — Dolomite, Sulphur, Carbon

For raw chemical and mineral powders, the 19×34 inch PP woven bag is the industry standard in India. This size is specifically suited for 25 to 40 kg fills of fine powders — a weight and volume range that is practical for manual handling at chemical plants, mineral processing facilities and agricultural input warehouses. The tighter fabric weave specification used in these bags (12×12 to 14×14 weave count) reduces fine particle leakage through the fabric, which is important for materials like dolomite powder, sulfur granules and carbon black.

Chemical companies use colour-coded PP bags for product grade identification — different colours or stripe combinations identify product purity levels, mesh sizes or application grades. This simple, cost-effective coding system eliminates the confusion and costly mix-ups that can occur when multiple similar-looking white bags are stored together in a warehouse or loading area.

Full Colour vs Colour Strip PP Bags — Which to Choose?

The choice depends on your priority. Full colour PP bags — where the entire fabric is woven from coloured tapes — provide the strongest visual identity and are the preferred choice for agricultural mandi bags where your brand colour needs to be visible from a distance in a crowded trading environment. Full colour bags also carry the most brand authority — they signal that you have invested in your packaging identity.

Colour strip bags — side strips or center strips on a white bag — are a more cost-efficient option for chemical and industrial powder packaging where the primary goal is product grade coding rather than retail brand building. The white body of the bag provides maximum readability for printed product information, while the colour strips provide the necessary visual differentiation between product types. For businesses managing multiple product grades or concentrations, a systematic colour strip coding system is the most practical and economical approach.
