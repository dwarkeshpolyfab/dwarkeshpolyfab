Frequently Asked Questions

What is anti slip coating on bags?

Anti slip coating is a special surface treatment applied to polypropylene bags that creates friction and prevents bags from sliding over each other during stacking.

Are anti slip bags required for export?

Many international buyers require anti slip bags for safe palletized shipments. It reduces cargo shifting during sea and road transport.

Can anti slip bags be printed?

Yes. Custom brand printing is available on anti slip bags with up to 6 colors.

What industries use anti slip bags?

Anti slip bags are used in rice, grain, fertilizer, chemical and export industries where palletized stacking is required.
