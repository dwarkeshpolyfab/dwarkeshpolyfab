Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between PP bags and BOPP laminated bags?

PP woven bags are plain polypropylene fabric bags. BOPP laminated bags have an extra BOPP film layer on top that gives glossy finish and high quality printing.

Can I print my brand logo on BOPP bags?

Yes. We provide up to 8 color high definition printing with your brand name, logo and product details on BOPP laminated bags.

Are BOPP laminated bags suitable for rice packaging?

Yes. BOPP laminated bags are widely used for branded rice packaging due to their superior print quality and moisture protection.

What finish options are available?

We offer glossy, matte, gold laminated and silver laminated finish options for BOPP bags.
