Frequently Asked Questions

What is a valve bag?

A valve bag has a small valve opening at the top for automatic filling machines to fill cement or powder material quickly without spillage.

Are cement bags compatible with automatic filling machines?

Yes. Our valve bags are designed to work with standard automated cement filling machines used in cement plants.

Can I get inner liner in cement bags?

Yes. We provide PE liner inside the bag to prevent cement dust leakage and moisture entry.

What GSM is best for cement bags?

We recommend 120 GSM to 140 GSM polypropylene fabric for standard 50 KG cement valve bags.
