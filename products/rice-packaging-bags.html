Frequently Asked Questions

Are rice packaging bags food safe?

Yes. All our rice packaging bags are made from food grade polypropylene material that is safe for direct food contact.

Can I get my brand printed on rice bags?

Yes. We offer full custom printing with your brand name, logo, rice variety details and product information.

What sizes are available for rice bags?

We manufacture rice packaging bags in 5 KG, 10 KG, 25 KG and 50 KG. Custom sizes also available.

Which is better for rice — PP bags or BOPP laminated bags?

For plain storage BOPP laminated bags are better as they provide moisture protection and premium print quality for retail branding.
