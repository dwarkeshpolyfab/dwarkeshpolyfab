What Makes Jumbo Box Bags a Special Category?

Jumbo box bags are not a standard size or weight product. They are a fully customised packaging solution built around the exact shape, size and handling requirement of your plastic product. Unlike regular polybags that are made in standard sizes, every jumbo box bag order is designed from scratch — width, height, depth, fabric weight, closure type and printing are all decided based on what you are packing.

This makes them the ideal choice for plastic manufacturers, plastic product distributors and traders who deal with products that do not fit into standard packaging — plastic strips, jars, trolley bags, cans, bottles and similar items that need a strong outer bag that holds its box shape during packing, stacking and transport.

Features of Jumbo Box Bags

  • Fully customised width, height and depth as per your product dimensions

How Customisation Works

Since every plastic product is different in shape and size, we do not have fixed dimensions for jumbo box bags. The process is simple:

  • Step 1 — You send us the dimensions of your product or the space needed inside the bag
  • Step 2 — We suggest the best bag size, GSM and closure type for your product
  • Step 3 — We share a sample before bulk production so you can verify fit and quality
  • Step 4 — After sample approval, bulk order is produced and dispatched

Simply WhatsApp us a photo of your product and tell us how many pieces you want to pack per bag — we handle everything else.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a jumbo box bag?

A jumbo box bag is a fully customised polypropylene woven bag with a rigid box shape — square or rectangular base with straight sides — designed to pack bulky, lightweight or odd shaped plastic products like jars, bottles, strips and trolley bags in an organised and stackable way.

Can I get any size made for my plastic product?

Yes. Jumbo box bags are 100% custom made. You share your product dimensions or send a photo on WhatsApp and we manufacture the bag to exactly fit your requirement.

Is this bag suitable for plastic strip bundles?

Yes. Box bags are excellent for plastic strip bundles as the rigid box structure keeps the strips organised and prevents them from spreading or tangling during transport and storage.

Can handles or lifting loops be added?

Yes. We can add side handles or lifting loops to jumbo box bags to make them easier to carry, lift with forklift or hang during storage — depending on your handling requirement.
