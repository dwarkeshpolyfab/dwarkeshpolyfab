Frequently Asked Questions

Are your rice bags food grade and safe?

Yes. All our rice packaging bags are manufactured from virgin food grade polypropylene that is completely safe for direct food contact and approved for rice storage.

Can I get my brand printed on rice packaging bags?

Yes. We offer full custom printing with your brand name, logo, rice variety, weight and contact details in up to 6 colors on all rice bags.

Which bag is best for retail rice packaging?

BOPP laminated bags with glossy or matte finish are best for retail rice packaging as they provide premium appearance and high definition print quality.

Do you supply to rice exporters?

Yes. We supply export quality rice packaging bags with anti slip coating and strong stitching suitable for international shipment standards.
