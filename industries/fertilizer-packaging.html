Frequently Asked Questions

Can fertilizer bags withstand chemical exposure?

Yes. Our fertilizer bags are made from chemical resistant polypropylene that safely stores all types of chemical and organic fertilizers without degradation.

Can safety instructions be printed on fertilizer bags?

Yes. We print NPK ratio, handling instructions, safety warnings and brand details with up to 6 color printing on fertilizer bags.

What GSM is recommended for 50 KG fertilizer bags?

We recommend 100 GSM to 120 GSM polypropylene fabric for 50 KG fertilizer bags to ensure optimum strength during loading and transport.

Are UV stabilized bags available for outdoor storage?

Yes. UV stabilized polypropylene fertilizer bags are available for companies that store bags in outdoor or semi-outdoor conditions.
