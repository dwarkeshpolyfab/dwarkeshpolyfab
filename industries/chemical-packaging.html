Frequently Asked Questions

Are PP bags safe for chemical packaging?

Yes. Polypropylene is chemically inert and does not react with most industrial chemicals, acids and alkalis making it a reliable choice for chemical packaging.

Can hazard and safety symbols be printed on chemical bags?

Yes. We print GHS hazard symbols, safety instructions, chemical name and handling warnings on industrial chemical packaging bags.

Are jumbo bags available for bulk chemical storage?

Yes. We manufacture PP jumbo bags from 500 KG to 1000 KG capacity for bulk industrial chemical and mineral storage and transportation.

Is PE inner liner necessary for all chemicals?

PE inner liner is recommended for fine powder chemicals, hygroscopic materials and products that must not contact the woven fabric directly.
