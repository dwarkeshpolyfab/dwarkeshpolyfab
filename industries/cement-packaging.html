Frequently Asked Questions

What is a valve bag and why is it used for cement?

A valve bag has a small self-sealing opening at the top for automated filling. Cement plants use it because it allows fast dust-free filling and seals automatically after filling is complete.

Do your cement bags work with automatic filling machines?

Yes. Our cement valve bags are manufactured to standard dimensions compatible with most automated cement filling and packing machines used in Indian cement plants.

Is inner liner mandatory for cement bags?

Not mandatory but highly recommended. PE inner liner prevents moisture from entering the bag and keeps cement dry and free flowing during storage.

What bag type is best for dry mix mortar?

Open mouth PP woven bags with PE inner liner work best for dry mix mortar as they allow easy filling, safe storage and reseal options on site.
