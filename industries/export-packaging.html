Frequently Asked Questions

Why are anti slip bags important for export packaging?

Anti slip bags prevent palletized cargo from shifting during sea freight and truck transport. Many international buyers and freight forwarders require anti slip bags for safe container loading.

Can export markings and country of origin be printed on bags?

Yes. We print all required export markings including product name, country of origin, net weight, batch number and buyer details on export packaging bags.

Do your bags meet international export packaging standards?

Our bags are manufactured from virgin polypropylene to consistent quality standards. We can provide material certificates and specification sheets for export buyers on request.

Which is better for export — PP bags or BOPP laminated bags?

BOPP laminated anti slip bags are the best choice for export as they combine moisture protection, brand quality printing and anti slip surface needed for international shipping.
